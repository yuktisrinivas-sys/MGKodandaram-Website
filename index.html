About

M. G. Kodandaram is a retired Indian Revenue Service (IRS) officer who served as Assistant Director at the National Academy of Customs, Excise & Narcotics (NACEN), Bengaluru, from 1981 to 2018 — a distinguished career spanning nearly four decades in service to the nation.

Today, he continues to serve as an Advocate and Consultant, and is recognized as a Master Trainer of GST for the Central Board of Indirect Taxes & Customs (CBIC), Government of India. His expertise spans GST, Customs, Central Excise, Service Tax, and a wide range of legal disciplines.
